The mother of the sheep Lily was lying over the bed and couldn't sleep. The lamb passed away, and the mother of the sheep shed tears silently, 
thinking of him in her heart. At this time, there was a sheep knocking on the door. It was the postman Lucy!	

Then the postman Lucy gave her a package and the mother sheep received the biggest shock! Inside was a tiny lamb and the mother sheep shed tears of joy. 
She could finally take care of another lamb again!

But after several days of taking care of this little lamb, Lily realized that she still felt sad because this is not her baby any more. She felt struggling
because she did not want to abandon this baby.

But anything is better than nothing. She can't have another baby and the tiny lamb is her only hope. She decides to hide this secret to tiny lamb to pretent she is lamb real mother.
